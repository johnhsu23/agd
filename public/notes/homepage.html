NOTE: The NAEP responding scales for music and visual arts range from 0–300. Please note when interpreting these results that NAEP survey questionnaire results are intended to place results in context. They cannot be used to infer cause–and–effect relationships between student characteristics and achievement.
SOURCE: U.S. Department of Education, Institute of Education Sciences, National Center for Education Statistics, National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP), 2008 and 2016 Arts Assessments.
