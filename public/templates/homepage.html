Eighth–graders' scores in music and visual arts, as well as percentages taking arts courses, unchanged since 2008

Average scores for eighth–graders were not significantly different between 2008 and 2016. Students who reported taking courses in music and visual arts scored higher, on average, than those not taking such classes. While the percentages of students who reported taking music and/or visual arts classes did not change significantly, lower percentages of students reported engaging in some music or visual arts activities outside of school between 2008 and 2016. Students were assessed separately on responding to existing works of music and visual art and creating their own original artwork.
