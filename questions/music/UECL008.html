[MUSIC PLAYER]

For question 8, you are to write an ending for the rhythmic pattern you see below. You will hear it played two times. After you hear the rhythmic pattern, write an ending to the pattern in the empty measures printed in your test booklet. The music that you write should make the rhythm sound finished. You may use notes or rests in your answer, but do not copy any of the measures that are already used in the music. Make sure that the ending that you write has the correct number of beats.

Solution:

This item measures two different skills: the ability to use the correct notation and the ability to write an appropriate ending. This scoring guide refers to the student's ability to write an appropriate ending.

Score and Description

DEVELOPED

Student's ending is musically and rhythmically satisfying.

INADEQUATE

Student's ending is not musically and rhythmically satisfying, or copies measures 1 and 2.

Sample Responses

DEVELOPED - Student Response

This response was judged to provide a satisfying ending to the beginning of the rhythmic pattern.

INADEQUATE - Student Response

This response, although it had no technical errors with respect to the use of musical notation, failed to provide a musically conclusive and satisfying ending to the rhythmic pattern.
