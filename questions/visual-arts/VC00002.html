Score and Description

ACCEPTABLE

The student gives two accurate identifying characteristics of charcoal.

PARTIAL

The student gives only one identifying characteristic of charcoal, e.g., dark shade.

UNACCEPTABLE

The student does not give identifying characteristics of charcoal.

Sample Responses

ACCEPTABLE - Student Response

This student shows very good knowledge of charcoal as a medium. The student is able to express him or herself in terms of what the artist could accomplish with the charcoal, as seen in the Kollwitz self-portrait.

PARTIAL - Student Response

This student also refers to the fact that the harder you press with charcoal, the darker the mark, as is visible in the Kollwitz portrait. It is not entirely clear what the second part of the response is referring to, but in any case charcoal can be used to draw a straight line.

UNACCEPTABLE - Student Response

This student does not describe characteristics of the medium used to make the self-portrait, but instead offers an interpretation of what is happening in the artwork.
