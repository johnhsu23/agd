Achievement Levels by Student Groups
Percentages of fourth-grade students at or above Proficient higher for most student groups compared to 2009

In 2015, the percentages of fourth-grade students who performed at or above the Basic and Proficient levels in science were higher for most student groups compared to 2009, the previous assessment year.

In 2015, the percentages of students who performed at or above Proficient were higher for all racial/ethnic groups with reportable results except for American Indian/Alaska Native students. In comparison to 2009, there were also higher percentages of male and female students, students eligible and not eligible for the National School Lunch Program, and students attending city, suburban, town, and rural schools who performed at or above the Basic and Proficient levels in 2015.
