Overall Score Results
Eighth-graders scored higher in science compared to 2011, 2009

In 2015, the average science score for eighth-grade students was 2 points higher compared to 2011, the previous assessment year. The average science score for eighth-grade students increased by 4 points compared to the first assessment year in 2009.
