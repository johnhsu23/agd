Scores by Content Area
Eighth-grade students scored higher in all three science content areas compared to 2011, 2009

The NAEP science assessment comprises three content areas: physical science, life science, and Earth and space sciences. Compared to 2011, the previous assessment year, average scores for eighth-grade students increased in all three content areas in 2015. Average scores were also higher in all three content areas in comparison to 2009, the first assessment year.
