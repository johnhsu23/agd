Scores by Percentile
Science scores not significantly different for lower-, middle-, and higher-performing twelfth-grade students compared to 2009

NAEP reports scores at five selected percentiles to show the progress made by lower- (10th and 25th percentiles), middle- (50th percentile), and higher- (75th and 90th percentiles) performing students. In 2015, science scores for twelfth-grade students at each of the five selected percentiles were not significantly different compared to 2009, the previous assessment year.
