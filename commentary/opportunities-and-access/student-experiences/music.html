Student and School Questionnaires
Explore results related to students' opportunities and access

A NAEP survey questionnaire was given to students who participated in the NAEP arts assessment. This questionnaire collected contextual information that helps put student achievement results into context and allows for meaningful student group comparisons.

Students’ experiences with learning about music are not limited to school. Therefore, the music portion of the 2016 arts assessment included a questionnaire focusing on students’ opportunities to access and engage in music both in and outside of school, as well as a questionnaire for school administrators focusing on resources and school demographics. Explore the music student and school survey questionnaires and learn more about NAEP survey questionnaires including how NAEP ensures the privacy of questionnaire respondents.

Interpreting the results

  • NAEP survey questionnaire results are intended to place results in context. They cannot be used to infer cause-and-effect relationships between student characteristics and achievement.
  • Many factors may influence student achievement, including educational policies and practices, available resources, and the demographic characteristics of the student body. Learn more about interpreting NAEP arts results.
