Student Group Responding Score Trends
Hispanic students scored higher in music; male students scored lower compared to 2008

In 2016, the average responding score in music for eighth-graders was 6 points higher for Hispanic students. The average score for male students was 5 points lower compared to the previous assessment year in 2008. Average eighth-grade responding scores in music in 2016 did not change significantly for other reported student demographic groups compared to 2008.

Compared to the previous assessment year in 2008, the racial/ethnic makeup of eighth-grade students in 2016 changed. For example, in 2016 the percentage of White eighth-grade students was lower compared to 2008, while the percentage of Hispanic students was higher.
