Student Group Score Trends
Hispanic students scored higher in music and male students scored lower compared to 2008

In 2016, the average responding score in music for eighth-graders was 6 points higher for Hispanic students and 5 points lower for male students compared to the previous assessment year in 2008. Average eighth-grade responding scores in music in 2016 did not change significantly for other reported student demographic groups compared to 2008.
