Student Group Score Trends
Responding scores in visual arts for most selected student groups not significantly different compared to 2008

In 2016, average eighth-grade responding scores in visual arts did not change significantly for most reported student demographic groups compared to 2008, the previous assessment year. The average responding score in visual arts in 2016 for students eligible for the NSLP was 5 points higher than in 2008.
