Student Experiences
Opportunities and Access in Music – 8th grade

Students’ opportunities to access and engage in music happen both inside and outside the classroom. Therefore, the music portion of the 2016 arts assessment included separate student and school survey questionnaires. Students answered survey questions about their music learning experiences and participation in music activities. School administrators answered survey questions about the availability of music education. Explore the music student and school survey questionnaires and learn more about NAEP survey questionnaires including how NAEP ensures the privacy of questionnaire respondents.

You can view selected results below. You can also use the NAEP Data Explorer to view results from the student and school questionnaires and see how students in the nation responded, as well as their average scores on the music portion of the arts assessment.

Interpreting the results

  • NAEP survey questionnaire results are intended to place results in context. They CANNOT be used to infer cause-and-effect relationships between student characteristics and achievement.
  • Many factors may influence student achievement, including educational policies and practices, available resources, and the demographic characteristics of the student body. Learn more about interpreting NAEP arts results.
