Responding Scores by Percentile
Highest-performing eighth-grade students scored lower in visual arts in 2016 compared to 2008

NAEP reports scores at five selected percentiles to show the progress made by lower- (10th and 25th percentiles), middle- (50th percentile), and higher- (75th and 90th percentiles) performing students. In 2016, eighth-grade students exhibited a range of visual arts responding scores, from 106 for lower-performing students at the 10th percentile to 189 for higher-performing students at the 90th percentile. Students at the 50th percentile had a visual arts responding score of 151. Scores for eighth-grade students at the 10th, 25th, 50th, and 75th percentiles were not significantly different compared to 2008, the previous assessment year. In 2016, the score of 189 at the 90th percentile was lower, compared to 2008.
